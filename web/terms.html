Booking and Cancellation Policy

· Upon confirmation of your booking a 50% deposit to the value of total accommodation is required.

· A deposit can be made by direct deposit into our bank account or credit card facilities.

· Refund of deposit (less cancellation fee) will be given for notice of cancellation up to 21 days prior to arrival date (Except Easter & Christmas reservations where 30 days' notice is required).

· A reservation is officially cancelled when the guest contacts Sharlynn by the River by phone on 07 4096 5884.

· Transfer of accommodation reservations dates will not incur any penalty when such transfer notice is received 21 days or more prior to arrival date (30 days for Easter & Christmas).

· A $80 cancellation fee applies to all cancellations.

· Gift vouchers are non-refundable and subject to our Booking and Cancellation Policy (Please remember to check the expiry date/conditions on your voucher).

We recommend guests consider travel insurance protection, particularly for longer stays.
